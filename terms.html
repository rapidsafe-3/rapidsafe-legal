Terms & Policies

RapidSafe Transit Solutions

Updated: May 2026

1. Nature of Service

RapidSafe is a technology platform that connects parents with independent auto-rickshaw drivers. RapidSafe does not own vehicles or employ drivers directly. We provide GPS tracking, attendance logging, and payment facilitation.

2. Pricing & Fees

Parents are charged a flat monthly subscription fee, which includes a 2% Platform & Technology fee. Driver payouts are subject to a 12% RapidSafe commission (covering platform access and gateway processing). Payments are securely processed via Razorpay.

3. Cancellation & Refund Policy

• Subscriptions: Monthly transit subscriptions are prepaid. Cancellations made mid-month are non-refundable for the current billing cycle.
• Physical ID Cards: Orders for Physical Smart ID cards (₹249) can be cancelled within 24 hours of placing the order for a full refund. Once shipped, cards are non-refundable.
• Refund Processing: Approved refunds will be credited back to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.

4. Limitation of Liability

While RapidSafe provides tracking tools, the physical transportation is provided by independent drivers. RapidSafe is not liable for traffic delays, accidents, or the physical actions of third parties.
