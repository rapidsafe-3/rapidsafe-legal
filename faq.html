How do I track my child's auto?

Once the driver scans the QR card, go to the Home dashboard and tap the 📍 Track Auto button to view live GPS.

How do payments work?

Parents pay a flat monthly subscription securely via Razorpay (UPI, Card, or Netbanking). Drivers receive their earnings directly to their verified bank accounts.

What if my child is sick?

Tap the 'Mark Leave' button on your dashboard. The driver will be instantly notified to skip your stop for the day.
