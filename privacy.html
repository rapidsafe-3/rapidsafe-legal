Privacy Policy

How we protect your data

Updated: May 2026

1. Data Collection

We collect essential data to ensure student safety. This includes parent contact numbers, student names, designated addresses, driver DL/RC details, and live GPS coordinates.

2. Live Location Tracking

Driver location is only tracked when the driver is toggled "Online" and actively on a route. This data is temporarily shared with the designated parent and is not sold to third parties.

3. Payment Data

All payment processing is handled securely by Razorpay. RapidSafe does not store your credit card numbers, UPI PINs, or raw banking passwords on our servers.

4. Your Rights

You may delete your profile or your child's data at any time via the app dashboard. Account deletion permanently removes your personal history from our active databases.
